About MedAlert

Ada is an AI-powered healthcare platform that offers a symptom checker to help users understand their symptoms and potential health conditions. Here's how the Ada symptom checker works:

1. User inputs their symptoms: Users can input their symptoms into the Ada platform via their website or mobile app. Ada uses natural language processing (NLP) to interpret the user's description of their symptoms.

2. Ada asks follow-up questions: Based on the user's symptoms, Ada asks additional questions to help narrow down the possible causes. These questions may include questions about the user's medical history, family history, lifestyle, and other factors that may impact their health.

3. Ada provides a personalized assessment: After gathering information from the user, Ada provides a personalized assessment of the potential health conditions that may be causing their symptoms. The assessment includes information on the likelihood of each condition, as well as recommendations for next steps, such as seeking medical attention or self-care.
